Free Workshop

The Laugh Class invites you to attend our free comedy workshop where you will learn the art and science of stand up comedy - in this 60 minute workshop you will learn the following

  • Why people laugh
  • the 3 ways to make make people laugh
  • How to win over any crowd
  • How to build a set
  • How to feel comfortable on stage 
  • Crowd work
  • Dealing with hecklers
  • What to do to start a career in comedy
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them
  • and much much more
Matt Taylor

Matt Taylor is a regular perfomer on the Los Angeles comedy club circuit and can be seen headlining shows at the Comedy Store, Ice House, and Improvs.

As a comedian, Matt has written for and appeared on MTV's Parental Approval, has a comedy special on HGTV, and has been profiled in the New York Post, New York Times, and NY-1.

As a teacher, Matt has worked with over 1200 comedians of all levels from complete novices to pros working on their Netflix special.

Matt has an undergraduate degree in trumpet from Carnegie Mellon University and a masters from New York University.

Questions & Answers

How do I attend an online workshop?

Click the button at the bottom to register - when you are finished we will send you a confirmation email that includes the URL of the workshop and the password to get in.  This workshop is hosted on Zoom  so please make sure to find a quiet place to enjoy the workshop.  We highly recommend headphones as well.   We will include all of this information and more in the confirmation email

I have never done comedy before - is this for me?

Yes! This workshop is perfect for anyone looking to try stand up comedy but isn't sure how to start or what to do. 

Can I bring in any jokes I have written?

Yes! If you have any jokes already written bring them and we'll show you how to apply the rules to the material you already created.   

How long have you been teaching? 

I have been teaching 16 years now and have had over 1200 students.  Many of my students are now working professionals and no I do not feel old at all.  Just proud.  Did that sound believable?

Is this really free? Like 100% free?

Yes!  There is absolutely no fee at all.  If you wish to donate after the workshop we will have a link available, but there is no pressure to do so.  I hate when people do that.   We also offer a class that is 4 weeks long so if you feel that you want to study further you have the option.
