The Laugh Class

The Laugh Class is a method for learning stand up comedy in a fraction of the time it would take you to learn it on your own.   Reserve your seat at this free workshop to see for yourself.

Topics Covered


  • Why people laugh
  • the rules all jokes follow
  • 2 ways to write a joke
  • how to break down jokes
  • how to beat hecklers
  • how to start a career
  • ... and more

You will learn

  • how to break down the complexities of humor and translate them into an incredibly simple process that you can use both on stage and off
  • stage-tested techniques on how to deliver a successful set of jokes,

  • how to build rapport and trust with an audience, and how to capture interest and make others laugh on command
